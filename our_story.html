Our Beginning

Our name comes from an afternoon in the autumn of 2023 in Berlin. When the climbing wines had strikingly changed in color, their red leaves reminded us stories of our ever changing Planet and the brave life in it, some stories evoked nostalgia, others hope, some felt cozy, others intense. As we kept gathering, it became evident that we all wanted a production company focused on documentary films, impact campaigns and educational audiovisual projects.

Our moviemaking underpins an understanding of storytelling as a creative experience between the communities and all other life forms on the Planet. We work at the crossroads of climate justice and culture, we want to be your place to foster conversation, inform, move, provoke and empower.

We are critical and tender.

Our drive is pressed into service on the realization of audiovisual projects and community organizing. We employ humoristic overtones too. Our perspectives are anti-colonial, feminist, queer and (im)migrant. We seek a shift in consciousness and practices to create healthier and reciprocal relationships among people and other species on the Planet through filmworks, impact campaigns and public programming.
